🧬 Reference: What happens during DNA repair?

🟢 HDR Pathway (30% Success Rate)

Homology-Directed Repair. The cell precise-copies the sequence from the provided donor template. Results in perfect, high-fidelity genome editing.

🔴 NHEJ Pathway (70% Mutations)

Non-Homologous End Joining. The cell's emergency mechanism. Unable to process the break carefully, it rapidly glues the DNA ends, frequently losing or adding random letters.
